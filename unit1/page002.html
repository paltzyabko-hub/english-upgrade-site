Reading Strategy #1

Read Smart, Not Hard
🎯 Your goal:
Learn how to understand long English texts without translating every word.

Good readers do not always read every word from the beginning to the end. First, they look at the title, pictures, headings and questions.

This helps them understand what the text is probably about before they read carefully.

📖 Skimming

Read quickly to understand the main idea.

🔎 Scanning

Look for specific information: names, dates, numbers and keywords.
💡 Remember: First understand the main idea. Then search for details.

How to Read Like a 5 Units Student

🎧 Audio

Good readers scan the page, think about the topic, connect ideas and answer using evidence from the text.

📚 Vocabulary

Words: scan, predict, evidence, connect, main idea, strategy, clue, confidence.

📖 Grammar

Use Present Simple for habits and facts. Use Present Continuous for actions happening now.

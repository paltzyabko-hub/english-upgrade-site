🧭 The 4-Step Strategy

S

SCAN

Look at the title, pictures, subtitles, bold words and questions. Do not read the whole text yet.

Question: What is this text probably about?
T

THINK

Activate what you already know. Good readers bring their own knowledge to the text.

Question: What do I already know about this topic?
C

CONNECT

While reading, look for relationships: cause and effect, contrast, examples and conclusions.

Useful words: because, however, therefore, for example, finally.
A

ANSWER

Answer only after you understand the question. Go back to the text and use evidence.

Rule: Do not answer from memory if the text can prove the answer.

❌ Common Mistakes

  • Reading every word before looking at the questions.
  • Translating the whole text into Russian or Hebrew.
  • Getting stuck on one unknown word.
  • Guessing without checking the text.
  • Copying long sentences instead of answering clearly.

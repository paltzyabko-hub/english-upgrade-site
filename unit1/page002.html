Reading Strategy #1

Read Like a 5 Units Student: scan first, think actively, connect ideas, then answer with evidence.

🎯 Today's Goal

After this lesson, you will know how to approach a long Bagrut-style text without panic.

  • Find the main idea quickly.
  • Ignore words that are not important.
  • Use questions before reading.
  • Support answers with evidence from the text.

🧭 SCAN → THINK → CONNECT → ANSWER

S

SCAN

Look at the title, pictures, subtitles, bold words and questions. Do not read every word yet.

Ask: What is this text probably about?
T

THINK

Activate what you already know. Good readers use background knowledge.

Ask: What do I already know about this topic?
C

CONNECT

While reading, notice how ideas are connected: cause, contrast, examples and conclusions.

Look for: because, however, therefore, for example, finally.
A

ANSWER

Read the question carefully, go back to the text and use evidence.

Rule: Do not answer from memory if the text can prove the answer.

❌ Common Mistakes

  • Reading every word before looking at the questions.
  • Translating the whole text.
  • Getting stuck on one unknown word.
  • Guessing without checking the text.
  • Copying long sentences instead of answering clearly.
